Cities Skylines Props

This page shows standard props in Cities: Skylines, including DLCs. The props are organized by Prop Collection.

In each gray box, the first line is the name of the prop. The second line is the service and sub-service (often 'None').

Cities Skylines supports prop variations. This means that the game selects the appearance of the prop randomly from a set of alternatives.
For example, Billboard_3D_variation can be either Rotating_squirrel, Rotating_morelloscone or Clown.
Below, props that have variations are marked with 'Has variations'.

New: Interactive feature provided by aubergine18 (Thanks!).
You can click on the gray boxes to create contents for skip.txt, for use with Loading Screen Mod. You can also click on the headings (Prop Collections).
You find the skip list at the bottom of this page.
